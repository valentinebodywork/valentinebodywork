About Me

My name is Jenny Valentine, and I am a massage therapist working in the SF Bay Area. I practice with an integrative deep-tissue approach designed to be therapeutic and easy to receive.

After nearly a decade working in technology, I was weary of the office and I needed to find a hands on approach to helping others. I sought out a way to connect during the isolation of the pandemic and found my passion in bodywork.

I attended the SF School of Massage and Bodywork, where I studied an anatomically focused curriculum based in western modalities. After graduating in June 2022, I stayed close to the school by enrolling in the TA program, helping to train the next generation of bodyworkers.

Today I work with all kinds of clients, and I specialize in serving the disabled and queer communities.
